Who am I ?

I am Tatiana Andreichuk, teacher, translator and just a person who is in love with French and who is ready to share her knowledge with all people who are interested in. I am practicing individual lessons for all levels: from beginners who want to learn how to read, to write and to understand elementary things in French to advanced people who just want to improve their knowledge more and to find out the smallest details of this language. I believe that my profession contributes to the development of people in general and to me personally. Because when I am teaching someone - I am improving myself. The best proof of my achievements is the success of my students, you can read their reviews in «Feedback». The feature of character that accurately describes me is insistence, but also I am kind and understanding =) I try to make me lessons in the form of a "friend-teacher / friend-learner", not in the form of a "teacher / learner".

Who is it for?

If you’re а beginner

We will learn how to write, to read simple texts or announcements, and then how to express your thoughts in simple phrases, to understand the essence of what is heard. We will also learn the necessary basic vocabulary, how to talk about yourself, to ask simple questions and to answer them.

You take a trip

Each of our classes will include a thematic vocabulary while modeling of the various situations that can be found abroad. Then you will be able to communicate and to solve the problems while passing your passport and customs control, filling in a form, booking and visiting restaurants or use of transport etc.

You live in a French-speaking country and want your professional growth

We’ll study the most necessary daily phrases and the most common grammatical constructions that are directly used by the population of French-speaking countries, overcome language barriers and increase the vocabulary needed, while discussing the most common topics and situations in everyday life.

You know French, but you want to improve your skills

During the lessons, we will listen to the contemporary music, watch modern French movies, read books, magazines and articles, watch French blogs in which we will pay attention to the smallest details and discuss them.

Don’t learn French, just live it!
